Project Overview

Tiknwin is a mobile and web platform designed for game enthusiasts to predict the outcomes of their favorite games. Earn points for correct guesses and win exciting prizes through our daily, weekly, and monthly contests.

Key Features

  • Game Predictions: Predict outcomes before matches and earn points.
  • Membership Options: Choose between free and paid memberships with exclusive benefits.
  • Prize Distribution: Redeem your points for prizes through Amazon and Flipkart.
  • Secure ID Verification: Ensure safe and fair gameplay with PAN and Aadhar verification.
  • Monetization: Enjoy a seamless experience with integrated ads and affiliate marketing.

How It Works

  1. Create an account and choose your membership type.
  2. Predict game outcomes before the match starts.
  3. Earn points based on your correct predictions.
  4. Redeem your points for prizes!
